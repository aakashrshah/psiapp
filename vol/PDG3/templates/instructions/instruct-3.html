Instructions

Here are a few tips to keep in mind while playing:

1) There is always a 'BEST' choice so please only choose one box. If you can't tell which box is better for getting a white marble then try to make your best guess.

2) Wait until you see both of the boxes on the screen before you make your choice. if you choose a box before you see both boxes you may have to choose again. If you have to choose again please do not switch your choice.

3) The size of the marbles do not influence which marble will fall out of the boxes. A single marble will randomly fall out of the box that you choose regardless or the number of small or large marbles in the boxes.

4) After a few choices you will get a chance to take a short break. You can take as long of a break as you need. When you are ready to resume, just click on the 'next' button.

5) All of the marbles are worth the same amount regardless of size. You will win the game if you collect a lot of white marbles regardless of their size. You still win if you collect a lot of small white marbles or a lot of large white marbles.
