Instructions

In this game, you will try to collect WHITE marbles.

On the screen you will see two trays like the ones below but there will be both red and white marbles in those trays.

The marbles in each of the trays will be poured into the boxes.

The contents of the BLUE TRAY tray will go in the BLUE BOX and the contents of the GREEN TRAY tray will go in the GREEN BOX.
