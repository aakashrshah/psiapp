Instructions

Once the trays have been emptied, the boxes will be shaken in order to randomize the marbles and you will see this image:

When you see this image you can choose a box to take a marble from.

If you think the BLUE BOX on the left is better for getting a white marble then press the 'Z' key.

If you think the GREEN BOX on the right is better for getting a white marble then press the 'M' key.

Once you have made your choice a marble will be randomly drawn from the box you chose. Remember, this marble is chosen randomly so it could end up being red or white and your job is pick the box that you think is best for getting a WHITE marble.
